Guide to aquire your "LUID" :-
Go to any "Edit Manifest" page, and right click anywhere on that page. Click on "Inspect Elements" and inside the new window go to the "Console" tab and paste the following code. 
console.log(document.querySelector("#_fid_90").value);
then just simply copy and paste the number (which would be something like "-63384563") inside the field above.
